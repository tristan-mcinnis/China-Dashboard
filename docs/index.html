                    About

                    I'm Tristan McInnis, and I built China Snapshot to read China the way it reads itself: in real time, on its own platforms. Most China coverage reaches you second-hand and a day late. This one pulls straight from the source, so you can see what state media is pushing, what people are arguing about online, and where markets and policy are moving.

                    I grew up in Hong Kong, studied Public Policy at Peking University, and have been in Shanghai since 2013, where I'm Managing Partner at Inner Chapter. Watching the country change from the ground, not from a feed, is what got me into this work. It still shapes how I read every signal here.
